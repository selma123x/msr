About Master Roof Solutions

Awarded Flat Roofing Company in Harrow, London

Master Roof Solutions Ltd provides its best services to clients and believes in long-term business relationships built on quality and trust.

We develop and deliver best-in-class flat roofing services across London with our highly skilled and trained tradespeople. Our team includes experts in waterproofing, torch-on felt, EPDM membrane, GRP fibreglass, and liquid roofing systems. Our professional approach to quality makes us the best choice in London for all flat roofing services — residential, commercial, and industrial.

We only specify industry-leading materials including Radmat, BauderTEC, and Danosa — the same brands used on London's best-built roofs — backed by full manufacturer warranties.
